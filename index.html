1. Overview

Falmera ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Falmera mobile application collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our service.

Falmera is a mobile application that provides dream interpretations and coffee fortune readings using AI technology. By using Falmera, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email Address: Used for account authentication and communication
  • Username: Chosen by you for display purposes within the app
  • Profile Picture (Optional): If you choose to upload one

b. User Content

To provide our services, we process:

  • Dream Descriptions: Text you provide for dream interpretation
  • Coffee Fortune Images/Descriptions: Content you submit for fortune readings
  • User Comments: Optional feedback on fortune interpretations

c. Technical Information

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (type, operating system version)
  • App usage statistics and performance data
  • Crash reports and error logs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To authenticate your account and secure access
  • To process your dream descriptions and coffee images through AI analysis
  • To improve and personalize your experience
  • To communicate with you about service updates
  • To monitor and analyze app usage and performance
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues

4. Data Processing and Third-Party Services

a. Google Firebase

We use Google Firebase services for:

  • Authentication: Secure user account management
  • Firestore: Cloud database for storing user data and content
  • Storage: Secure file storage for profile pictures
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve our service

Firebase data is stored on secure Google Cloud servers. View Firebase Privacy Policy

b. Google Gemini AI

We use Google's Gemini AI to analyze and interpret:

  • Dream descriptions for meaningful interpretations
  • Coffee fortune images and patterns

Your content is processed by Gemini AI solely for providing the interpretation service. View Google Privacy Policy

c. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share information with:

  • Service providers (Firebase, Gemini AI) necessary for app functionality
  • Legal authorities when required by law

5. Data Storage and Security

a. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Google Cloud infrastructure:

  • Account information and user content in Firestore database
  • Profile images in Firebase Storage
  • All data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS

b. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Account data: Until you delete your account
  • User content: Until you delete it or your account
  • Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymized

c. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and monitoring
